rDrafts is one of a few apps created by GroupSRC. We've also created the following apps that make Reddit a little easier to use.

EasyAMA

EasyAMA is similar to rDrafts, except that it is specifically designed for AMAs. We created this app to give users a template for creating a successful AMA. The app walks the user through the AMA planning process and encourages them to submit an AMA that is informative, valuable, and verified.

Visit easyama.groupsrc.com

EasyUnsub

EasyUnsub is for redditors that want to quickly unsubscribe from multiple subreddits. A common use-case is when you have created a new account and want to quickly unsubscribe from the default subreddits..

Visit easyunsub.groupsrc.com
